- 3/5 Games for Losers & Winners Finals.
- 3/5 Games for Grand Finals.

- Default Game Settings
- Winner must keep the same team, Loser may switch.
- Winner and Loser of a game may switch team order each game.
- Accepted Stages:
    - Riverside Space.
    - Training Stage.
