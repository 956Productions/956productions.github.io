## Details

## Tournament Format

- Double Elimination.
- 2/3 Rounds per Game.
- 2/3 Games per Set.
- 3/5 Games for Losers & Winners Finals.
- 3/5 Games for Grand Finals.

## Tournament Rules

- Default settings.
- Random stage select (players may opt for an agreed stage).
- Winner must keep character, Loser may switch.
- Game breaking glitches are banned.
