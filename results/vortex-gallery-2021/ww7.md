## Tournament Format

- Double Elimination
- Pools
    - 2/3 Rounds per Game.
    - 2/3 Games per Match.
- Top 8\*
    - 2/3 Rounds per Game.
    - 3/5 Games per Match.
- \*Top 8 rules will be Top 3 only if total player attendance after disqualifications is 16 or less.

- Default game settings.
- Wakuwak7 is the required romset.
