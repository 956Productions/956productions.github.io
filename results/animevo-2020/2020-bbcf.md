## Tournament Format

- Double Elimination.
- 2/3 Rounds per Game.
- 2/3 Games per Set.
- 3/5 Games for Losers & Winners Finals.
- 3/5 Games for Grand Finals.

## Tournament Rules

- Default Settings.
- Accepted Stages:
    - Colosseum Silent
    - Old Gate
    - Altar
- Winner must keep character, Loser may switch.
- Game breaking glitches are banned.
